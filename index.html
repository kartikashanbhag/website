Our Story

Three Decades of Manufacturing Excellence

From humble beginnings in 1994 to becoming a trusted name in precision manufacturing, our journey is built on quality, reliability, and continuous innovation.

Established in 1994, Shan Enterprises has evolved from a 225 sq.ft facility with a single drilling machine into a state-of-the-art 7,200 sq.ft manufacturing plant. Today, we specialize in precision-engineered components for the electronics industry, serving both EMS companies and OEMs across India.

Our Vision

To be a global leader in precision-engineered thermal management and component manufacturing, trusted for innovation, quality, and reliability.

Our Mission

We deliver excellence in heat sinks, precision machining, and anodized components. By deepening expertise and expanding capabilities, we provide customized solutions that strengthen our customers' products and long-term success.

What We Do

Core Competencies

Five specialized manufacturing capabilities backed by precision equipment and decades of expertise

VMC Machined Components

High-precision multi-axis machining with ±10µm tolerance. Ideal for complex geometries in aluminum, brass, and stainless steel.

  • Tolerance: ±10 µm
  • Finish: Ra < 1.6 µm
  • Materials: Al, Brass, SS

Precision Pressed Parts

High-accuracy pressing for thin metal components with tight tolerances across various materials.

  • Tolerance: ±50 µm
  • Thickness: 0.5-5 mm
  • Materials: Al, Cu, Brass, MS, SS

Moulded Components

Precision injection moulding for engineering plastics with excellent dimensional accuracy and surface finish.

  • Tolerance: ±10 µm
  • Size: Up to 300×300 mm
  • Materials: Nylon, ABS, PC, PP

Customized Heat Sinks

Our specialty: Extruded and machined heat sinks with superior thermal conductivity. Over 100 in-stock designs with custom solutions available.

  • Thermal κ: ≈ 200 W/m·K
  • Materials: Al, Cu & Alloys
  • Finish: Ra ~ 0.8 µm
Core Expertise

High Quality Anodising

Sulphuric anodizing with exceptional corrosion resistance. Available in natural, black, and red finishes with multiple surface textures.

  • Thickness: 10-20 µm
  • Hardness: Up to 400 HV
  • Finishes: Matte, Satin, Glossy
